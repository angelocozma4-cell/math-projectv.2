Polynomials

What is a Polynomial?

A polynomial is an expression made up of variables and coefficients, combined using addition, subtraction, and multiplication. Example: 3x2 + 2x - 5.

  • Degree: The highest exponent of the variable.
  • Coefficient: The number in front of each variable.
  • Constant term: A number without a variable.
